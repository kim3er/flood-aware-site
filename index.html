Flood Aware App

With the flood aware app, you can track water levels in your local area. Data is sourced from the Environment Agency.

The app is free to download and use, and will also report on flood alerts/warnings within 5km of your location.

Currently available for iOS, Android and Chrome. To download the app, please click on one of the links above.
